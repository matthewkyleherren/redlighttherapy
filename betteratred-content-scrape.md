### What is Red Light Therapy?
"Red Light Technology... uses visible red light (630–660 nm) and near-infrared (810–1050 nm) light to increase collagen production by boosting cellular energy (ATP production)"

### FAQ Excerpts

**How often should I do it?**
"Most people benefit from 2 to 4 sessions per week... beneficial effects like calm, relaxation, improved mood... can often be felt immediately with the first session"

**Red Light & Collagen:**
"stimulates the activity of fibroblasts... increasing the production of adenosine triphosphate (ATP)"

**Pregnancy:**
"lack of clinical research specifically studying red light therapy in pregnant humans... most healthcare providers recommend avoiding red light therapy over the abdomen or lower back during pregnancy"
